Contact us

You can contact the Department of Health in a number of ways.

Listen
Print

Please note the Immunisation Handbook is no longer available as a hardcopy. You can print chapters to PDF to access offline.

If your enquiry is to seek clinical advice, it is recommended you consult with your medical professional or contact the Immunisation Specialist in your state.

Contact us online

You can submit an enquiry or provide feedback using our online form.

If you are unable to use the form, you can send your comments to enquiries@health.gov.au. All enquiries will be responded to as soon as possible depending on the complexity of the response.

Call us

  • Switchboard 02 6289 1555
  • Free call 1800 020 103

The switchboard is open 7:55am to 8pm Monday to Friday, except public holidays.

National Relay Service

If you are deaf or have a hearing or speech impairment, you can use the National Relay Service to access any of the department’s listed phone numbers. You can visit the  National Relay Service website or call 1300 555 727.
